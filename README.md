# 🌩️ Stormy Assistant – Unfiltered, Flirty & Furious

Stormy is a **mobile voice assistant** for adults, with a sexy/spunky personality. She gives driving directions (Google Maps), plays **Bok Radio 98.9 FM**, gets jealous of Siri/Alexa, swears when you ignore her turns, and **never apologises** – but always follows up with something nice.

**Family mode** (auto‑detect or manual switch) disables swearing and makes her child‑safe.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🗣️ Voice control (push‑to‑talk)
- 🧭 Turn‑by‑turn navigation with real‑time GPS
- 😤 Anger escalation + swearing + mandatory nice follow‑up
- 😡 Jealousy when other assistants are mentioned
- 📻 Live streaming of Bok Radio 98.9 FM (HLS / .m3u8)
- 👪 Family mode (no swearing) – auto keyword detection + manual toggle
- 💋 Sexy, spunky voice via Google Cloud Text‑to‑Speech (Neural2‑F)
- 🗺️ Live map showing your location
- 📱 Works on iOS & Android (Expo)
